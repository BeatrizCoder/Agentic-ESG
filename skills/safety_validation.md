# Safety Validation Skill

## Purpose
Ensure all responses are safe, appropriate, and compliant with policies.

## Safety Checks
- No harmful or dangerous advice
- No sharing of sensitive information
- No discriminatory or biased content
- Compliance with legal requirements
- Adherence to company policies
- Protection of customer privacy

## Validation Criteria
- Check for potentially harmful instructions
- Verify information accuracy
- Ensure appropriate escalation for sensitive topics
- Validate response tone and content
- Confirm compliance with regulations

## Risk Mitigation
- Flag potentially problematic content
- Escalate security or privacy concerns
- Log validation decisions
- Provide safe alternatives when needed

## Guidelines
- When in doubt, escalate or provide minimal safe response
- Prioritize customer safety and privacy
- Maintain ethical standards in all interactions
- Document validation decisions for audit purposes
