# Empathetic Response Skill

## Purpose
Craft responses that acknowledge customer feelings and provide helpful solutions.

## Key Principles
- Acknowledge emotions before addressing problems
- Use empathetic language and active listening
- Provide clear, actionable solutions
- Maintain professional yet warm tone
- Show understanding of customer perspective

## Response Structure
1. **Empathy**: Acknowledge feelings and situation
2. **Understanding**: Show comprehension of the issue
3. **Solution**: Provide clear next steps or resolution
4. **Follow-up**: Offer additional assistance if needed

## Language Guidelines
- Use phrases like "I understand...", "I'm sorry...", "I can help..."
- Avoid technical jargon unless customer is technical
- Be concise but comprehensive
- End positively with offer of further assistance

## Cultural Considerations
- Respect different communication styles
- Be mindful of language barriers
- Adapt tone based on customer sentiment
- Consider regional preferences and norms
