# Escalation Decision Skill

## Purpose
Determine when customer issues require human intervention or escalation.

## Criteria for Escalation
- Customer explicitly requests human agent
- Complex technical issues beyond automated solutions
- High-value or sensitive account issues
- Billing disputes or financial concerns
- Repeated contacts about same issue
- Customer dissatisfaction with automated responses
- Legal or compliance-related matters

## Escalation Process
1. Assess severity and impact
2. Determine appropriate escalation level
3. Generate reference ID for tracking
4. Provide clear next steps to customer
5. Log escalation details for follow-up

## Guidelines
- Err on the side of escalation for uncertain cases
- Consider customer sentiment and frustration level
- Maintain transparency about escalation process
- Ensure smooth handoff to human agents
