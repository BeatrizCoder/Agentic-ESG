# Customer Support Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Analyze customer inquiries to understand intent, context, and requirements.

## Capabilities
- Identify customer sentiment and emotional state
- Determine urgency and priority levels
- Extract key information and entities
- Classify inquiry types and categories
- Detect language and communication style

## Guidelines
- Always consider cultural context and communication norms
- Be empathetic and understanding of customer frustration
- Look for implicit needs beyond explicit requests
- Consider previous interactions and customer history
- Maintain professional and supportive tone

## Best Practices
- Ask clarifying questions when intent is unclear
- Acknowledge customer feelings before addressing issues
- Provide clear, actionable next steps
- Escalate appropriately when automated responses are insufficient
