# Account Access

## Reset Your Password
Step-by-step to reset your password:

1. Go to the login page at: https://app.supportai.com/login
2. Click "Forgot your password?" below the login button
3. Enter your registered email address and click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your inbox for an email from noreply@supportai.com
   (check spam/junk if not received within 2 minutes)
5. Click the "Reset Password" button in the email
   (link expires in 30 minutes)
6. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters,
   must include 1 number and 1 special character)
7. Confirm your new password and click "Save"
8. You will be redirected to login with your new password

If you don't receive the email:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Make sure you're using the correct email address
- Try again after 5 minutes
- Contact support if still not received after 10 minutes

Reset link: https://app.supportai.com/forgot-password

## Unlock a Locked Account
Your account locks after 5 failed login attempts.

Automatic unlock:
- Wait 30 minutes — account unlocks automatically
- Then try logging in again

Manual unlock via email:
1. Go to: https://app.supportai.com/unlock-account
2. Enter your registered email
3. Click the unlock link sent to your email
4. Try logging in again

If account remains locked:
- Contact support with your registered email
- We verify your identity and unlock within 1 hour
- For security, we cannot unlock without identity verification

## Update Your Profile
To update account information:

1. Log in at: https://app.supportai.com/login
2. Click your avatar/name in the top right corner
3. Select "Account Settings" or "Profile"
4. Edit the fields you want to change:
   - Name, phone, address: edit and click "Save Changes"
   - Email: requires confirmation to new email address
   - Password: requires current password to change
5. Click "Save Changes" to confirm

To change your email:
1. Go to Settings → Email Address
2. Enter new email and current password
3. Confirm link sent to NEW email address
4. Old email stays active until new one confirmed

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
To enable 2FA for extra security:

1. Go to: https://app.supportai.com/settings/security
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Choose method: SMS or Authenticator App
4. Follow setup instructions
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

To disable 2FA:
1. Go to Security Settings
2. Click "Disable 2FA"
3. Enter your current password to confirm

## Account Hacked or Unauthorized Access
If you suspect unauthorized access:

IMMEDIATE STEPS:
1. Change your password immediately:
   https://app.supportai.com/forgot-password
2. Enable 2FA if not already active
3. Check recent activity at:
   https://app.supportai.com/settings/activity
4. Revoke all active sessions:
   Settings → Security → "Sign out all devices"
5. Contact support immediately — this is high priority

Our security team responds to hacking reports within 1 hour.
Reference our security page: https://app.supportai.com/security
