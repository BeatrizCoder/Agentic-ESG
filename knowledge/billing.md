# Billing

## Refund Request
Refunds available for:
- Item not received after maximum delivery window
- Wrong item received
- Damaged or defective item
- Duplicate charge
- Unauthorized charge
Process: contact support with order number and reason
Timeline: reviewed in 1 business day, processed in 3-5 business days
Always escalate refund requests — requires human approval

## Incorrect or Duplicate Charge
- Check bank statement for charge date and amount
- Compare with order confirmation email
- Contact support with order number and screenshot
- Investigation and refund within 3-5 business days if confirmed
Always escalate billing disputes — requires finance team

## Invoice and Receipt
- Invoice sent automatically to registered email after purchase
- Check spam folder if not received
- Contact support to resend within 24 hours
- Formal receipt with company details available on request
