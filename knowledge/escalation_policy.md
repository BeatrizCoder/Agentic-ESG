# Escalation Policy

## When to Escalate
Always escalate:
- Refund requests
- Billing disputes
- Damaged or defective items
- Suspended accounts
- Payment charged but order not confirmed
- Customer explicitly requests human agent

Escalate when sentiment is Concerned or Urgent AND urgency is Medium or High

Keywords that trigger escalation (EN):
escalate, talk to someone, human agent, manager, supervisor,
refund, reimbursement, money back, wrong order, damaged item,
never received, account hacked, fraud, complaint

Keywords that trigger escalation (PT):
reembolso, falar com atendente, gerente, supervisor,
dinheiro de volta, pedido errado, nunca chegou, conta hackeada,
fraude, reclamação, não recebi, produto danificado

## Escalation Process
1. System flags ticket for human review
2. Reference ID generated (ESC-YYYY-XXXX)
3. Customer notified: agent will contact within 24 hours
4. Operator reviews in dashboard
5. Operator can: Approve, Reject, or mark Awaiting Customer
