# General Support

## Return Policy
Keywords: return, devolução, devolver, política de devolução, reembolso, refund, exchange, troca, troco, estorno

Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of delivery.

Conditions for return:
- Item must be unused and in original packaging
- Must have proof of purchase (order confirmation email)
- Digital products and customized items cannot be returned
- Final Sale items are non-returnable

How to return:
1. Contact support with your order number
2. We send a prepaid return label to your email
3. Pack the item and drop off at any carrier location
4. Refund processed within 3-5 business days after receipt
   OR exchange item shipped within 2 business days

Return window exceptions:
- Damaged or defective items: contact us immediately, no time limit applies
- Wrong item received: contact within 7 days

For exchanges: same process as returns, just specify
"exchange" and the desired item when contacting support.
Para trocas: mesmo processo, informe "troca" e o item desejado ao contatar o suporte.

## Order Tracking
Keywords: track, rastrear, rastreamento, rastreio, acompanhar pedido, tracking, onde está meu pedido, how to track

To track your order:
1. Check your order confirmation email for tracking number
2. Visit the carrier website (listed in your email)
3. Enter your tracking number
4. For real-time updates: log into your account at
   app.supportai.com and go to "My Orders"

Para rastrear seu pedido:
1. Verifique o e-mail de confirmação do pedido para o número de rastreamento
2. Acesse o site da transportadora (indicado no seu e-mail)
3. Digite o número de rastreamento
4. Para atualizações em tempo real: acesse sua conta em app.supportai.com e vá em "Meus Pedidos"

If tracking shows "delivered" but you didn't receive it:
- Check with neighbors or building reception
- Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
- Contact support if still missing after 24 hours

## Order Cancellation
Keywords: cancel, cancelar, cancelamento, cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement.
After 1 hour: contact support — we try to intercept
before shipping but cannot guarantee cancellation.
If already shipped: wait for delivery then return.
Pedidos podem ser cancelados em até 1 hora após a realização.
Após 1 hora: contate o suporte — tentamos interceptar antes do envio.
