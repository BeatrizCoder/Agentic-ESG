# Order Issues

## Order Not Arrived
If an order hasn't arrived within the estimated window:
- Check tracking number in confirmation email
- Visit carrier website with tracking code
- If status shows delivered but not received, check with neighbors
- If no update for 5+ business days, contact support
Standard delivery: 5-7 business days
Express delivery: 1-2 business days
If lost: we reship or refund at no cost

## Wrong Item Received
- Do not use or open the incorrect item
- Take photos of what was received
- Contact support with order number and photos
- Free return pickup arranged
- Correct item shipped within 2 business days
- Refund available within 3-5 business days

## Damaged or Defective Item
- Take clear photos of the damage
- Do not discard packaging
- Contact support within 48 hours
- Options: free replacement or full refund
- Items damaged during shipping covered by delivery guarantee

## Tracking Not Updating
- Wait 24-48 hours, carriers batch updates
- Check carrier website directly
- If no update after 5 business days, we open inquiry
- Response within 24 hours
Escalate if: no update after 5 business days
