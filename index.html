When you access the platform, a temporary token is created and stored only in your browser (localStorage). It expires automatically after 24 hours. This token is NEVER stored in our database.

Your IP address is processed by our hosting provider (Render) only for rate limiting (max 10 req/min). It is NOT stored in our database.
